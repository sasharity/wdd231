Site Plan: LagosToDo

Site Name

Name: LagosToDo

This name was chosen because it is short, memorable, and clearly reflects the purpose of the website: helping people discover fun things to do in Lagos.

Optional domain availability: lagostodo.com (availability not required for class)

Site Purpose

The purpose of LagosToDo is to serve as a helpful guide for tourists and fun-seekers in Lagos by showcasing different activities, destinations, and events. The site will include information on restaurants, beaches, art centers, nightlife spots, and more.

Scenarios

  • What are the best beaches to visit in Lagos?
  • Where can I go out at night in Lagos?
  • What family-friendly activities are available for kids?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #0077b6 (used for headings, buttons, and links)
  • Accent Color: #fcbf49 (used for highlights and call-to-action elements)
  • Background Color: #f5f8ff (light, clean background)

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif – used for all titles and section headings
  • Body Font: 'Segoe UI', sans-serif – used for paragraphs and content
